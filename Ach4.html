Cadet Information

Topic: Drill of the Element as an Element Leader (not in ranks)

Conditions: Provide the cadet with a list of the commands shown below (see Questions sheet) and allow the cadet to refer to them during the course of the test. Instruct the cadet to lead the element in drill, completing all commands in sequence. The element should consist of at least 3 cadets.

Passing Score: 80% — must fulfill at least 4 out of the 5 acceptable standards satisfactorily.

Note: The element's ability to perform the commands is NOT relevant to this test, only the cadet's ability to call commands properly is being evaluated.

Questions

Calls commands on the correct foot (when the foot corresponding to the direction of movement strikes the ground).

Calls commands of execution two steps after calling the preparatory commands.

Calls commands loud enough for the element to hear them.

Calls commands clear enough for the element to understand them.

Proper inflection (raising) used.

Calls commands decisively, with snap and a sense of 'Go!'

Maintains good military bearing.

Calls cadence or halts and restarts the element, if the cadets fall out of step or lose alignment.

Completes all assigned commands.
