Cadet Information

Topic: Squadron Drill

Conditions: At least two cadets who can competently command a flight in a squadron formation are needed. Provide the cadet with the commands sheet and allow them to refer to it during the test. Instruct the cadet to form the squadron in line and lead the squadron in drill, completing all commands in sequence.

Passing Score: 76% — must perform at least 13 out of the 17 commands satisfactorily.

Note: The flights' ability to perform commands is NOT relevant, but flight sergeants must know supplementary commands. If the cadet being tested gives the command correctly, mark them Satisfactory even if the flight sergeants don't correctly give supplementary commands.
